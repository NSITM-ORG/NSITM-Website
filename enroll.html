No-Refund Policy

All fees paid to Nextserve School of Information Technology and Management are strictly non-refundable. Attendance Policy
Not yet acknowledged
▼

Students are required to attend a minimum of 80% of scheduled classes per cohort. Consistent absenteeism without prior notification may result in removal from the programme without refund. Online students are expected to attend live sessions where scheduled or complete recorded sessions within 48 hours of release.

Students are expected to treat all instructors, staff, and fellow students with respect.
